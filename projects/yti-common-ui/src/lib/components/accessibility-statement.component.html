Accessibility statement

The operator of the PZSI platform undertakes to make the websites of the PZSI platform accessible to all users of the platform in accordance with the Act on the Accessibility of Websites and Mobile Applications.

Compliance level

The websites of the PZSI platform are partially compliant with the Act on the Accessibility of Websites and Mobile Applications due to the exceptions listed below.

Since the platform is intended only for certain employees in the state administration who are engaged in the field of data management and/or semantic interoperability, and it is a small number of users and there was no perceived need for customized content, the platform operator ensured only partial compliance with the Accessibility Act websites and mobile applications.

The content is not specifically tailored for:

  • display of content for users of assistive technologies (screen readers, reading line),
  • keyboard navigation (for users who cannot use a mouse),
  • accessibility of content in Slovenian sign language,
  • fitting images with alt text.

Feedback and contact information

Notification of possible cases of non-compliance of publications with the provisions of the Act on the Accessibility of Websites and Mobile Applications and requests to provide information in an accessible format can be sent by regular mail or email to the address of the operator of the PZSI platform. In your message, please include:

  • the nature of your accessibility problem,
  • the desired format in which you want to receive information, and
  • your contact information.

Izjava o dostopnosti

Upravljalec platforme PZSI se zavezuje, da bo vsem uporabnikom platforme omogočal dostopnost spletnih mest platforme PZSI v skladu z Zakonom o dostopnosti spletišč in mobilnih aplikacij.

Stopnja skladnosti

Spletna mesta platforme PZSI so delno skladna z Zakonom o dostopnosti spletišč in mobilnih aplikacij zaradi spodaj navedenih izjem.

Ker je platforma namenjena le določenim zaposlenim v državni upravi, ki se ukvarjajo s področjem upravljanja podatkov in/ali semantične interoperabilnosti, in gre za manjše število uporabnikov ter ni bilo zaznanih potreb po prilagojenih vsebinah, je upravljalec platforme zagotovil le delno skladnost z Zakonom o dostopnosti spletišč in mobilnih aplikacij.

Vsebina ni posebej prilagojena za:

  • prikaz vsebin za uporabnike podpornih tehnologij (bralniki zaslona, brajeva vrstica),
  • navigacijo s tipkovnico (za uporabnike, ki ne morejo uporabljati miške),
  • dostopnost vsebin v slovenskem znakovnem jeziku,
  • opremljanje slik z nadomestnim besedilom.
